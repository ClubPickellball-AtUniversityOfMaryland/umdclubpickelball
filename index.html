About Us

Welcome to the University of Maryland Club Pickleball! Our mission is to promote the sport of pickleball among students, faculty, and staff at the University of Maryland. We aim to foster a community of players who can improve their skills, compete, and enjoy the game together.

The club was founded in 2017 by Ben Johns. Since its inception, we have grown into a vibrant community of pickleball enthusiasts dedicated to spreading the joy and excitement of the game.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide students with the opportunity to learn and play pickleball, promoting physical activity, social interaction, and skill development.

Our Activities

  • Open play sessions on Tuesdays from 4pm to 10pm and Thursdays from 4pm to 8pm
  • Intra-club tournaments
  • Social events
  • Merchandise
  • Participation in intercollegiate competitions
